## Part I: The Operating Model

### What We Sell

**Done-for-you website templates** — not generic WordPress themes, but complete, niche-specific website packages that include:

1. A professionally designed, mobile-first website template
2. Pre-configured affiliate integrations (scheduling, payments, CRM, email marketing)
3. SEO-optimized copy and structure for the target niche
4. A setup guide that gets the buyer live in under 30 minutes

### Why This Works

Small business owners don't want to build websites. They don't want to learn code. They don't want to hire developers. They want to be **live today** with something that looks like they spent $5,000. We give them that for $199–$699.

The affiliate stack is where the business model compounds. Every template comes pre-wired with affiliate links to tools the business owner actually needs:
- Scheduling (Calendly, Acuity) — 15-30% recurring commission
- Payment processing (Stripe, Square) — referral bonuses
- Email marketing (Mailchimp, ConvertKit) — 20-30% recurring
- CRM (HubSpot free tier → paid) — 20% recurring
- Booking/Reservations (Resy, OpenTable) — per-lead fees

**Revenue Model:**
- Template sale: $199–$699 one-time
- Affiliate recurring: $20–$80/month per customer
- Service upsell (setup help): $99–$299 one-time

### The Math

To hit $10K MRR in 30 days:

| Scenario | Template Price | Sales Needed | Affiliate/mo per customer | Total Sales | Daily Sales |
|----------|---------------|-------------|--------------------------|-------------|-------------|
| Conservative | $299 avg | 28 templates | $30 avg | $8,372 + $840 affiliate = ~$9,212 | ~1/day |
| Moderate | $349 avg | 22 templates | $40 avg | $7,678 + $880 affiliate = ~$8,558 | ~0.75/day |
| Aggressive | $399 avg | 20 templates | $50 avg | $7,980 + $1,000 affiliate = ~$8,980 | ~0.7/day |
| Blended Target | $349 avg | 25 templates | $40 avg | $8,725 + $1,000 = ~$9,725 | ~0.85/day |

**The blended target: 25 template sales at $349 avg + $1,000 in affiliate MRR = $9,725+**

This means we need roughly **1 sale per day** with a healthy affiliate attach.

### Tier 1 — Launch Niches (Week 1)
These have the highest conversion probability and fastest speed-to-sale.

### Tier 2 — Growth Niches (Week 2)
Scale into adjacent verticals with proven template patterns.

| Niche | Template | Price | Why |
|-------|----------|-------|-----|
| **Pet Services** | Starter Pack | $249 | High search volume, passionate owners, low competition |
| **Fitness Studios** | Pro Template | $399 | Strong brand builders, scheduling + class booking affiliate stack |

### Tier 3 — Premium Niches (Week 3-4)
Higher price points, lower volume, but significantly better unit economics.

| Niche | Template | Price | Why |
|-------|----------|-------|-----|
| **Law Firms** | Premium Suite | $699 | Highest ARPU, strong need for professional online presence |
| **Medical/Dental** | Premium Suite | $599 | Compliance-aware templates, high trust requirement |

### Decision Framework: When to Cut vs Scale

**Scale signal:** >3.5% conversion rate, <$20 CPA, affiliate attach >50%
**Hold signal:** 2-3.5% conversion, testing window open
**Cut signal:** <2% conversion after 72 hours of qualified traffic, or CPA >$30

## Part V: The Technology Stack

- Next.js 16 + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS
- Real-time sales tracking via LemonSqueezy webhooks
- Agent intelligence layer (6 AI agents + Adaptive CSUITE)
- Mobile-first responsive design

### Sales Infrastructure
- **LemonSqueezy**: Primary payment processor + digital delivery
- **Gumroad**: Secondary channel for marketplace discovery
- **Skool**: Community + upsell funnel (starting Monday 3/17)

### Template Delivery
- Pre-built on modern stacks (Next.js, Astro, or clean HTML/CSS)
- Hosted on Vercel/Netlify with one-click deploy
- GitHub repo access included with purchase

### Data Pipeline
- LemonSqueezy webhooks → CSUITE dashboard
- Affiliate link tracking via UTM + platform dashboards
- Conversion tracking: Meta Pixel, Google Analytics 4
- CSUITE agent layer processes all data for recommendations

### AI Agent Layer
- 6 specialized agents (PRODUCT, GROWTH, FINANCE, PROTOCOL, BRAND, TALENT)
- Adaptive CSUITE meta-intelligence for cross-agent synthesis
- All agent recommendations logged with confidence scores
- Override tracking for recommendation accuracy calibration
